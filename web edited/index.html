Build Your Own Job Portal

ATS develops your own career site so you can post unlimited job ads for your company needs and improve your employment brand.

Comprehensive Talent Pool

Your job ads will appear on your career site, our job portal, as well as our media partners to obtain more candidates and develop your own talent pool. ATS also helps recruiters maintain communication with the candidates and show they’re interested, even when a role isn’t available.

Systematic Recruitment Process

ATS is a web-based system that can be customized with your recruitment flow. It allows you to track, sort, search and schedule an interview with the candidates.

Faster Recruitment Process

ATS can help you with your candidate selection issue as it organize itself into categories such as the top qualified the candidates you’ve interviewed, and the candidates you should give an immediate ofe.

Time and Cost Efficiency

ATS provides more efficient solution and cheaper cost compared with the standard job portal.

Accountable Report and Analysis

Through ATS, you can store all of your candidates' information in one system. ATS allows you to monitor hiring performance and reducing the cost of administration and paperwork to minimize manual data processing.
