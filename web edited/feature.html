Our Features





Job Posting Management

ATS provides job posting features to help you get more candidates. Each job ads appears on your own career site, Karirpad's job board, and over 750 media partners around Indonesia. So you shouldn't worry about getting the best candidate for each position.






Candidate Management

ATS helps recruiters sorting candidates quickly according to the requirements of the position. Thus you will have your own talent pool and can make offers to candidates based on specific keyword searches.






Process Management

Create and customize your recruitment flow. Recruiters can track and see the history of each recruitment process, so there is no double processing of candidates.






Report

The feature assists to collecting, preparing and reviewing summary reports from the whole database for recruiter in the specific period.






Tools

Special menu for recruiters to input data manually from referrals from employees.






Career Site

Candidates will apply your job ads through various channels which will be directed to your career site. You can use this to build your own database of candidates.
